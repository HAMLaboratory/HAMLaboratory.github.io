Solutions

Our complete solution doesn't end with just a portfolio of matrixes, diagrams, and numbers. It includes the installation and bring-up of our robots. More importantly, it deploys a system design allowing swarm operation and full autonomy of your robots. A coherent mapping technique provides the ideal navigation and control of your fleet of robots at the ease of your desktop, tablet, or phone.

Applications

Package Handoffs

Hermes is ideal at package handoffs or any repetitive movement of materials over a distance with significant throughput. The tape following technology can handle dynamic transportation, and the heavy duty build supports packages up to 500 kg making appliances, pallets, and liquids to be transported with ease. This would also cover applications including unloading/loading of inventory, raw materials handling, object inspection, and pallet handling.


Palletizing

Artemis' fixture on the flexible Mecanum drive is engineered to pickup and remove packages atop a platform such as a pallet, table, or another package. The fixture is armed with a strong hydraulic system handling flat-faced packages up to 40 kg. Recognizing a barcode, Artemis can interface with your database and can carefully palletize a pallet or depalletize a pallet.


Robot and Forklift Management

HAM View allows careful management of your warehouse resources. It tracks your robots, modified forklifts, drop-off locations, and pick-up locations with the help of live cameras and tracking devices. With a Google-Map like interface, you can dispatch forklifts or robots by selecting a pick-up location and drop-off location as you watch your bots move intelligently across the map.
