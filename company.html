HAM Laboratories

HAM Laboratories was founded in 2014 located at the tech hub in Waterloo, Canada. It pivoted from a research project into a company driven by knowledge, passion, and action. HAM Laboratories is striving to be the pioneer of truly intelligent and general purpose mobile robotics in Canada.

Founders

HAM Laboratories is built on the foundation of three Universiy of Waterloo graduates from Mechatronics and Mechanical Engineering. Like minded, they all had a question. Howcome robotics isn't adapted by all industries yet. What are the decision barriers that companies face. In active discussions and multiple meetings with industry leaders to answer this question, the three Engineers formed HAM Laboratories.
