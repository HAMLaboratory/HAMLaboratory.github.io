Hermes


Product Features

  • Coloured guide tape guidance that is invariant to ambient light
  • Heavy duty traction rubber wheels for concrete floors
  • Simple operation with low chance of failure
  • General purpose AGV
  • Manual override and emergency stop with proximity sensors
  • Suspension to handle uneven or rough floors
  • Optional 10cm platform lift
